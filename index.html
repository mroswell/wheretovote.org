Find out where to vote

There are several national polling place lookup tools, fueled by the "Google Civic Data API." Try them!

  • GoToThePolls.com (currently forwards to another URL on the Voting Information Project site)
  • an embeddable Voter Information Tool (You may use here, ore you can also embed into your own website)
  • The Google Civic Data API will be of interest to web developers.
